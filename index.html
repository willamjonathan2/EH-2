BACKGROUND


Thesedays, people are very busy with work. Some of them have workloads that force them to do overtime hours. Consequently, these people do not have extra time to do other activities or they have a choice to stop working. We have surveyed that there are lots of people who are busy working or doing other activities in this world. However, they also have parents to take care of. How are you able to manage the time between taking care of your parents or elderlies and your working time? It must be hard right?

HASH is an app that helps you to take care of your parents or elderlies.

HASH helps you to find the most compatible assistant for elderlies. There are many features in HASH, that ease you to order and track the assistant. Besides that, you are able to review the assitant after the service is done. Moreover, we also keep track of the history of your orders hence, if you are able to re-order again.
